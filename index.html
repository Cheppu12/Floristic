About

Dulquer Salmaan is an acclaimed Indian actor known for his versatile performances in the Malayalam film industry. Born on July 28, 1986, in Kochi, Kerala, Dulquer hails from a family deeply rooted in the entertainment industry, with his father, Mammootty, being a legendary actor. Dulquer made his acting debut in 2012 with the film "Second Show," which garnered him critical acclaim and marked the beginning of a successful career. He quickly rose to prominence with his charismatic presence and ability to portray a diverse range of characters with ease. Throughout his career, Dulquer has delivered several memorable performances in films such as "Ustad Hotel," "Bangalore Days," "Charlie," and "Kammatti Paadam," among others. His performances have earned him numerous accolades, including several Filmfare Awards and Kerala State Film Awards. In addition to his work in Malayalam cinema, Dulquer has also ventured into other film industries, including Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi, expanding his reach and further showcasing his acting prowess. With his charm, talent, and dedication to his craft, Dulquer Salmaan continues to captivate audiences and establish himself as one of the most accomplished actors in Indian cinema.
